The Magic Behind Your Route

We use a sophisticated algorithm that guarantees finding the most efficient path between any two stations. Think of it as a smart GPS that considers not just distance, but also cost and time efficiency.

Route Weight Calculation

Total Route Weight

W = αD + βC

Where:

  • W = Total Weight of the route
  • D = Total Distance (in kilometers)
  • C = Total Cost (in ₹)
  • α = Distance weight factor (0.6)
  • β = Cost weight factor (0.4)

Example Calculation

For a route with:

  • Distance = 100 km
  • Cost = ₹500

W = (0.6 × 100) + (0.4 × 500)

W = 60 + 200

W = 260
